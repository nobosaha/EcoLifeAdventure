About EcoLife Adventure

EcoLife Adventure is a lightweight, gamified website that helps users discover their daily environmental impact through a quick interactive quiz, friendly challenges, and instant tips. No signup, browser only, and free for all.

Why this matters (SDG alignment)

  • SDG 12 — Responsible Consumption: Encourages reduced single-use plastics and smarter consumption.
  • SDG 13 — Climate Action: Promotes transport & energy habits that lower carbon footprints.
